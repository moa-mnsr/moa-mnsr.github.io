ABOUT

These web poems were handcoded and written by Morgan Mansour (*1991, US). The site is a compilation of pieces from 2023-2025. The font is Whois Mono by Raphaël Bastide.

I (she/they) am currently working on a networked volume of texts while studying with the Klasse Digitale Grafik. I started a master's at HFBK Hamburg in October 2025, and have been living in Germany since 2016.

This first collection grew with abundant freshwater from Kristoffer Tjalve and gentle light from Laurel Schwulst. Many thanks to many friends whose careful eyes and warm voices have likewise helped guide these pages, poems, and me.
