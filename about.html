ABOUT

⌂

I (she/they) am a writer and media artist living with a potato cat on an island in a river in a port. I like swimming in libraries, hiding in pools, and learning to take care of vegetables.

⚘

These poem-sites grew with abundant freshwater and sunshine from Kristoffer Tjalve since last summer, and took root with the gift of lightness from Laurel Schwulst in late winter 2023.

This project will continue to expand into a longer garden / networked volume in an MFA with the Klasse Digitale Grafik @ HFBK Hamburg, starting in the fall of this year (2025).

Many thanks to many friends whose guiding eyes and warm voices have likewise helped grow these pages, poems, and me.
