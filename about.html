ABOUT

This collection of web poems was written and coded by Morgan Mansour.

I (she/they) am a writer and media artist living with a potato cat on an island in a river in a port. I like swimming in libraries, hiding in pools, and learning to take care of vegetables.

These sites grew with abundant freshwater and sunshine from Kristoffer Tjalve since last summer, and took root with the gift of lightness from Laurel Schwulst in late winter 2023. The font is Whois Mono by Raphaël Bastide.

This project will continue to expand into a longer garden / networked volume while studying a Master of Fine Arts with the Klasse Digitale Grafik @ HFBK Hamburg from the fall of this year (2025).

Many thanks to many friends whose guiding eyes and warm voices have likewise helped grow these pages, poems, and me.
